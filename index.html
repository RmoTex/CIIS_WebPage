System

Our Lab had been established from February in 2002, and we focus on using and innovating new technology on a variety of information systems and application research. The fields we are currently working on including but is not limited to the following topics：

(1) Wireless communication protocols
(2) OpenStack、Open Air Interface、Docker
(3) 4G LTE Small Cell
(4) 5G - Tactile Internet
(5) Multimedia systems
(6) Image Detection and Recognition System

Our lab not only has some high-speed equipment working for researches and simulations, but also high efficiency computing servers to implement new technology and new algorithms. All the members in CIIS Lab are expected to put theory into practice and make innovation.
